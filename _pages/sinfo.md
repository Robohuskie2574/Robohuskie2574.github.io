# Sponsor info and guidelines
Thanks for being interested in sponsoring us! Sponsors can get benefits such as a place on our website, your company name on out t-shirts, and your logo on our robot. You can [contact us]({{site.baseurl}}/Contact) for more info. Our team email is <medtronicrobohuskie2574@gmail.com>.

## Sponsor Benefits
  * Your logo on our website and robot
  * Your name on our t-shirts

## Logo guidelines
The website and logo can use the same logo or different logos.
### Your logo for the website:
  * Should be a high enough resolution to look good when scaled up, like when viewing the website on a large monitor.
  * Must not be taller than it is wide.
  * Should not be too wide. A logo more than 2.5-3 times it's height is not recommended for it may be too short when on the website.

### Your logo for the robot:
  * Should look good at a range of distances.
  * Should have a reasonably high resolution.

Please send us your preferred logo. If we are unable to get a preferred logo from you matching the criteria, we will try to find a suitable logo, which might not be the one you prefer.
